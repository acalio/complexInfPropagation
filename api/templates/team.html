Andrea Tagarelli

Associate
  • Bio Associate professor of computer engineering with the Dept. Computer Engineering, Modeling, Electronics and Systems Engineering, University of Calabria, Italy. He also obtained the Italian national scientific qualification to full professor in computer engineering in 2017. His research interests are in the areas of data mining, machine learning, network analysis, web and semistructured data management, information retrieval, and bioinformatics. He co-organized workshops on data mining topics in premier conferences in the field (ACM SIGKDD, SIAM DM, PAKDD, ECML-PKDD, and ECIR). He served as co-program-chair of the 2018 IEEE/ACM Int. Conf. on Advances in Social Network Analysis and Mining. Since 2015, he has been in the editorial board of Computational Intelligence Journal and Social Network Analysis and Mining Journal.
